## Full recording script

Quick LLM answers can sound confident, but confidence is not verification. They
can hallucinate facts, skip citations, miss fresh information, and leave you
with claims you cannot actually check.

DeepResearch is built for the moment when an answer has to be trusted. Upload
your knowledge base, enable web search, and get grounded answers with sources
you can trace.

Sign in, open the workspace, and pick up the research thread.

Now the user asks a real question: research the best positioning for a
multi-agent deep research platform, using uploaded pitch notes and market
documents. RAG and web search are on, so the answer can combine private context
with fresh evidence.

Behind the chat, the query enters a coordinated agent workflow. The Research
Orchestrator owns the quality gate. The Research Planner breaks the question
into subtopics, then focused researchers investigate sources, compare evidence,
and report what is certain, uncertain, or missing. Medior checks the evidence
before anything returns.

The researchers do not just dump notes into a summary. They challenge each
other, checking market signals, workflow pain, competitor claims, and source
quality inside Band.ai under Medior supervision.

When findings align, Medior packages the answer: citations attached, limitations
marked, weak claims visible. The Orchestrator does one final quality pass and
sends it forward.

The user receives a concise synthesis, not a guess: planned, cross-checked,
sourced, and ready to defend.
